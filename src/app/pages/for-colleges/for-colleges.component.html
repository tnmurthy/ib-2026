Engagement Models

Flexible approaches tailored to your college's needs and readiness

01

Awareness & Orientation Sessions

Start with introductory sessions for students, faculty, and management. We explain the ecosystem concept, share case studies, and help stakeholders understand the potential impact.

  • Duration: 1-2 days
  • Audience: Students, faculty, management
  • Outcome: Clarity on approach and benefits
02

Department-Level Pilots

Launch a pilot program with one or two departments. Test the ecosystem approach, refine delivery, and demonstrate results before expanding campus-wide.

  • Duration: One semester/academic year
  • Scope: Selected departments and year groups
  • Outcome: Proven results and learnings for scale-up
03

Full Campus Ecosystem Rollout

Implement the complete Innovat Bharat ecosystem across all years and departments. Build a comprehensive program with industry partnerships, startup support, and long-term mentorship.

  • Duration: Multi-year partnership
  • Scope: Entire college, all students
  • Outcome: Transformed institution with sustained impact
