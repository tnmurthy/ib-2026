Campus Ecosystem Program

All Years · All Streams

Our core offering — a structured, year-round parallel ecosystem built inside your college. It integrates mindset training, industry exposure, mentorship, and startup culture alongside regular academics. Delivered through weekly sessions, field visits, guest lectures, and project work that students can pursue without sacrificing their formal coursework.

  • Weekly sessions (2–3 hrs) integrated with college timetable
  • Dedicated mentors assigned to each batch
  • Semester-wise milestones and outcome tracking
  • Industry visits and live project assignments
  • Peer learning groups and startup ideation circles
📅 Full academic year 👥 1st – 4th year students 🎓 All engineering & commerce streams

Foundation & Mindset Series

1st Year Focus

A high-impact orientation and development series for first-year students. This program builds the mental foundation—career clarity, goal-setting, entrepreneurial thinking, and learning habits—before students get lost in semester cycles. It's the difference between 4 purposeful years and 4 wasted ones.

  • Career clarity workshops and self-discovery exercises
  • Introduction to entrepreneurship and startup culture
  • Communication, confidence, and soft skills training
  • Goal-setting frameworks and accountability systems
  • Exposure to alumni journeys and industry realities
📅 First two semesters 👥 1st year students 🎓 All streams
💼

Earn-While-You-Learn Internship Pathways

2nd Year Onwards

Real internships, real work, real income. We connect students from second year onwards with companies and startups where they contribute ~3 hours per day and earn stipends of approximately ₹10,000/month. This isn't shadowing—it's live project work that builds portfolios and reduces the financial burden on families.

  • Matched placements based on stream and interest
  • ~3 hrs/day commitment, compatible with college schedule
  • Stipends ranging ₹5,000–₹15,000/month (where feasible)
  • Mentored by company leads and Innovat Bharat advisors
  • Internship certificate and portfolio documentation
📅 Ongoing — semester-based cycles 👥 2nd year onwards 🎓 Tech, Commerce, Agriculture & more
🚀

Startup & Innovation Labs

3rd & 4th Year Focus

For students ready to build something. This program supports student teams in ideating, validating, and launching micro-ventures or social impact projects. Guided by experienced mentors, teams go through structured stages: idea exploration, market validation, prototype, and pitch. Some teams move toward incubation support.

  • Team formation with cross-domain collaboration
  • Structured innovation sprints (idea → prototype)
  • Mentorship from entrepreneurs and domain experts
  • Pitch preparation and investor/grant exposure
  • Pathway to incubation partnerships (IIT/IIM networks)
📅 Two semesters minimum 👥 3rd & 4th year students 🎓 All streams — cross-domain teams encouraged
🌐

Domain Expert & Industry Connect Series

All Years · Regular Cadence

A regular series of sessions, workshops, and field visits delivered by working professionals, entrepreneurs, and domain specialists. Not motivational speakers — real practitioners sharing real insights. Covers technology, agriculture, manufacturing, finance, healthcare, and more, depending on the college's regional industry context.

  • Monthly expert sessions with Q&A
  • Quarterly field visits to companies and factories
  • Domain-specific workshops (tech, agri, civil, commerce)
  • Networking sessions with local and national entrepreneurs
  • Access to Jagriti Yatra alumni and IIT/IIM mentor network
📅 Monthly/quarterly cadence 👥 All year students 🎓 Domain-specific tracks available
