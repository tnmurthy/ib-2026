Innovat Bharat invites experienced professionals and industry leaders to share their wisdom with the next generation of creators. We don't just want your time; we want your experience to help students avoid common mistakes and dream bigger.

🎓

Expert Masterclasses

Share your knowledge through guest lectures or workshops (online or in person).

🧭

Direct Mentorship

Guide student startup teams as they build their first products and business models.

📝

Project Reviews

Provide honest, professional feedback on student projects to make them "industry-ready."

🏛️

Sharing "Traditional Wisdom"

Help students blend modern technology with the solid business values that have built our nation.

Why Mentor With Innovat Bharat?

Build a Legacy: Use your years of hard-earned experience to shape the future leaders of "Bharat."
Fresh Energy: Stay connected with the raw energy and "out of the box" thinking of young, ambitious students.
Give Back to the Roots: Help students in Tier-3 towns get the same quality of guidance that is usually only available in big cities.
Shape the "Product Nation": Be a key part of the movement that turns India into a global hub for indigenous products.
Personal Growth: There is no greater joy than seeing a student you mentored turn a simple idea into a real-world business.
No One Walks Alone: Your guidance ensures that a young founder from a small town doesn't have to struggle alone.

How You Can Volunteer

💻

Share Your Skills

Spend a few hours a month teaching students practical skills like coding, digital marketing, or basic accounting.

🤝

Be a Student Buddy

Guide a small team of students as they work on their first project. Sometimes, they just need someone to talk to and share their ideas with.

📢

Spread the Story

Help us reach more people by sharing the success stories of our student-founders on social media or in your local community.

📍

Help on the Ground

If you live near one of our partner colleges, you can help us organize events, workshops, or "startup days" on campus.

🌐

Open Your Network

Introduce us to people who can provide projects, mentorship, or resources to our students.

📸

Document the Journey

If you are good at photography, writing, or making videos, help us capture the transformation happening in these colleges.

Why Volunteer With Us?

Be a Change-Maker: Directly help a student from a small town build a better future.
Learn and Grow: Work with a high-energy team and stay connected with fresh, new ideas.
Build Bharat: Contribute to the national goal of making India a "Product Nation" from the grassroots level.
Real Impact: See the immediate results of your help when a student earns their first income or builds their first product.
