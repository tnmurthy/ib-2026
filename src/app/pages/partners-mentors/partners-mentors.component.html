Mentor & Partner Networks

We draw from world-class networks to bring the best mentorship to ordinary colleges

🎒

Jagriti Yatra Network

India's largest youth entrepreneurship train journey that connects young changemakers with rural role models and social entrepreneurs. Our founder's fellowship gives us access to a nation-wide network of purpose-driven mentors and change agents.

🏫

IIT / IIM Alumni

Mentors from premier technical and management institutions who volunteer to bridge the gap between tier 1 and tier 3 education. Their networks open doors to internships, research projects, and startup incubation opportunities.

🏭

Industry Practitioners

Working professionals, startup founders, and domain experts across technology, manufacturing, agriculture, finance, and healthcare who engage with students through expert sessions, internship partnerships, and project mentorship.

🌱

Local Business Communities

Traditional business families and local entrepreneurs who bring generational business wisdom, real mentorship, and ground-level market understanding—things no classroom can replicate.

Become a Mentor or Partner

👤 Individual Mentor

Commit a few hours per month to guide students, deliver sessions, or review projects in your domain.

🏢 Industry Partner

Offer internship slots, live projects, field visits, or sponsored sessions to students across campuses.

🤝 Institutional Partner

Co-create programs, share resources, and collaborate to expand impact across regions.
